Before You Write

Common Questions

How long does a commission take?

Our average commission timeline is 5–8 months. Complex full-body silicone pieces can take up to 12 months. You'll receive regular updates via your client dashboard throughout.

What is the minimum commission price?

Commissions start at ₹85,000 for a 3/4 limb vinyl reborn. Full-body platinum silicone commissions begin at ₹1,60,000. A 40% non-refundable deposit secures your place.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes. We ship to 38+ countries. All dolls are packed in custom wooden crates. International shipping typically costs ₹18,000–₹35,000 depending on destination.

Can I visit the studio?

Studio visits are welcome by appointment only. Commission clients are invited for a mid-creation visit at the painting stage — an experience collectors describe as deeply moving.
