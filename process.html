Step by Step

From Clay to Life

01

Sculpting

Original clay sculpt created, then a platinum silicone mould. Each mould takes 2–3 weeks.

02

Genesis Painting

20–30 thin layers of heat-set paints. Baked between layers at 130°C for permanence.

03

Hair Rooting

Each strand individually needled into the silicone mould. 80–120 hours per doll.

04

Assembly & Cert

Glass-bead filling. Magnet pacifier. Numbered certificate of authenticity signed.
