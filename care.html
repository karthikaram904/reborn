Doll Care Guide

Your doll is crafted to last a lifetime. With the right care, she will remain beautiful for generations.

Essentials

Care at a Glance

🌡️

Temperature

Store at 18–24°C. Avoid direct sunlight which yellows silicone. Never freeze or place near heat sources.

🧼

Cleaning

Wipe gently with a damp cloth and mild baby soap. Never submerge. Pat dry and air for 24 hours.

✂️

Hair Care

Brush with a soft bristle brush. Never use heat tools. For tangles, apply a tiny drop of baby conditioner.

📦

Storage

Store in original box with acid-free tissue when not displayed. Avoid plastic bags which trap moisture.

Care Kit Included

Every doll ships with our studio care kit: soft brush, recommended soap sachets, acid-free tissue, and a laminated care card.

FAQs

Care Questions

Can I dress my doll in regular baby clothes?

Yes! Your doll fits standard newborn or preemie baby clothing. Avoid tight elastic cuffs which can create pressure marks. Pre-wash all new garments before dressing.

My doll has a sticky surface — is this normal?

Slight tackiness can occur in humid conditions. Dust lightly with pure corn starch baby powder using a clean dry brush. This restores the matte skin texture immediately.

Can I take my doll outdoors?

Brief outdoor use is fine. Avoid prolonged UV exposure which will yellow and degrade silicone. Keep in shade and use a UV-protective display case at home.

How do I fix a stain?

Apply 10% benzoyl peroxide acne cream to the stained area and leave in indirect sunlight for a few hours. This lifts most dye-transfer stains. For deep stains, contact the studio for a professional refresh.
