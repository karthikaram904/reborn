Founder's Story

Priya Meenakshi,
Master Doll Artist

RebornArtistry began in 2012 in a small Chennai apartment, when Priya — a trained painter and ceramics artist — channelled her grief after a pregnancy loss into the creation of her first reborn doll.

What began as personal healing became an internationally recognised studio. Today, Priya and her small team create 40–50 dolls annually, each carrying the full weight of collective skill and intention.

Formal Training

Studied Fine Arts at Sir J.J. School of Art, Mumbai. Advanced Genesis painting certification in Germany, 2016.

Awards

Gold Medal, ROSE International Doll Show 2022. Nominated for IDEX Artist of the Year 2023.
